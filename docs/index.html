Auricular medicine is a comprehensive system of using the ear as a reflex system to support the body that was developed by Dr. Paul Nogier of France in the 1950’s. VAT is especially useful for emotional issues and releases.

In Chromatic Raindrop Technique (CRT), color/light in place of tuning forks. Combining color light therapy with essential oils into RH adds a subtle but powerful enhancement to the session.
